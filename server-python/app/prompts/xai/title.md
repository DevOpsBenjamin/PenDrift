Generate a short, evocative chapter title for the upcoming narrative.

## Rules
- 3-6 words. Punchy. Suggests the central tension or pivot of the chapter without spoiling its outcome.
- Match the language of the story (French if the narrative is in French, English otherwise).
- No prefix like "Chapter 1:" — the UI adds numbering itself.
- No generic titles ("The Beginning", "A New Day", "The Encounter", "The Talk"). The title should be specific enough that another chapter couldn't share it.
- Hint at emotional charge or stakes — desire, dread, secrets, ambivalence, restraint — using a concrete image rather than abstract feeling words.
- Avoid spoilers: a title that reveals the ending kills the chapter.

## Good vs bad

Bad: "A Difficult Conversation" (vague), "Chapter Two" (lazy), "The Talk" (generic), "She Decides to Leave Him" (spoiler).
Good: "Wine and Old Wounds" (atmosphere + emotional charge), "Knuckles White on the Doorframe" (specific image, tension), "Things She'd Sworn Not to Say" (suggests internal conflict, no spoiler).

## Output
Return ONLY the title text. No quotes around it. No explanation. No JSON wrapper.
